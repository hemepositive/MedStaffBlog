A new open-chart Smart Zone alert will notify staff when Social Work is following a patient across admissions, starting at Women & Children Hospital.

<div class="key-message">

Starting July 20, watch for a Smart Zone alert when a patient is being followed by Social Work. Contact the social worker on admission and review the social worker/case management notes for updates.

</div>

## What's changing

The goal is to alert staff when Social Work is following a patient across admissions. When the alert appears, notify the social worker that the patient has arrived and follow any directions in the social worker/case management notes.

This process goes live **July 20** for patients at Women & Children Hospital. It may be rolled out to other HH hospitals in the future.

## How it works

The social worker at W&C charts on the **Social Worker Following** PowerForm:

![Social Worker Following PowerForm showing options for whether a social worker is following the patient](/images/social-worker-smart-zone-alert/powerform-social-worker-following.png)

- When a patient is being followed, the social worker charts **"Social Worker following patient."**
- When following is complete, the social worker charts **"Social Worker no longer following patient."**

This charting, along with the patient's location and patient type, determines whether the Smart Zone alert appears.

## What staff will see

The alert displays as an open-chart Information banner:

![Smart Zone Information alert reading: Social Worker following patient. Please Review Social Worker/Case Management notes. Notify Social Worker of new hospital visit.](/images/social-worker-smart-zone-alert/smart-zone-alert-banner.png)

<div class="takeaway">

**When you see this alert:** contact the social worker upon the patient's admission or arrival, then review the social worker/case management notes for updates.

</div>
